Team

In the Macroecology Lab, we have members from around the world, currently representing 5 countries. As students typically follow their own interests, we collectively research a range of taxonomic groups (amphibians, insects, mammals, fish), systems (terrestrial, aquatic, marine), and geographic scales (Japan, Asia, global).
